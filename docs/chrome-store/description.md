## English🔍 Smart Image Extraction

```text

Automatically finds every image on any webpage — including standard images, CSS backgrounds, responsive images, and high-resolution variants. Scans deep into Shadow DOM and iframes for complete coverage. Live monitoring detects new images as you scroll (Pro).

🎛️ Powerful Filtering & Sorting

Filter by size (Small/Medium/Large/XL/Custom), format (JPG/PNG/WebP/SVG/GIF), layout (Square/Landscape/Portrait/Panorama), or URL keywords. Smart grouping by Domain, Format, Size Range, or Tab (Pro). Sort by size, format, or natural order. Toggle "Show visible only" to focus on images currently on screen.

📥 Batch Download Images

Download individual images or batch download multiple images as a single ZIP file. Track download progress with a visual progress bar. Free users can download up to 30 images per batch; Pro users enjoy unlimited batch downloads.

🌐 Multi-Tab Extract

Extract images from multiple browser tabs at once. No need to switch tabs one by one — scan all open tabs simultaneously and download everything in one go. Perfect for research, shopping, or content curation across multiple pages.

🔎 Similar Image Detection

Automatically detect and group visually similar images using advanced perceptual hashing. Avoid downloading duplicates or near-identical variants. Saves time and storage by identifying redundant images before you download.

🔍 Reverse Image Search

Find where else an image appears online. Free users can search via Google Images. Pro users unlock 4 search engines: Google Images, TinEye, Baidu, and Yandex — giving you comprehensive coverage to track image usage, find higher resolutions, or verify authenticity.

✨ Batch Highlight

Select multiple images in the panel to highlight them all on the webpage simultaneously. Auto-scrolls to each highlighted image so you can quickly locate and verify them on the page. Ideal for reviewing large batches before downloading.

⭐ Image Collections

Save your favorite images to a local collection with custom tags and notes. Browse, search, and filter your saved images anytime. Batch export entire collections as ZIP files. Build your personal image library without leaving your browser.

🎨 Color Extract

Automatically extract the top 5 dominant colors from each image. View color palettes directly in the panel. Copy HEX color codes for design work, branding analysis, or creative projects.

🤖 AI Image Tagging

Generate smart tags for any image using AI — instantly know what's in each image without opening it. Tag individual images or batch-tag your entire selection at once. Filter and search images by their AI-generated tags. Free users get a monthly quota; Pro users enjoy unlimited tagging.

🦅 Export to Eagle

Send images directly to Eagle — the popular design asset management app. Each export includes the image, metadata, AI tags, and source URL so your Eagle library stays organized. Batch export all selected images at once. Free users can export up to 5 images; Pro users enjoy unlimited exports.

⚡ Batch Operations

Select multiple images and perform actions in bulk: batch favorite (add to collection), batch AI tag, and batch delete. No more one-by-one clicking — streamline your workflow when dealing with large image sets.

🌍 15 Languages Supported

Fully translated into 15 languages: English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, 日本語, Español, 한국어, Português, Français, Deutsch, Italiano, Русский, Nederlands, Polski, العربية, ไทย. Auto-detects your browser language — no manual setup needed.

🔒 Privacy First

Image Harvest processes everything locally in your browser. No images, URLs, or personal data ever leave your device. Optional anonymous usage statistics (event counts only — no URLs, no images, no identifiers) can be enabled in Settings → Help Improve; off by default.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+Shift+S (Cmd+Shift+S on Mac): Toggle Image Harvest

🌐 Website: https://image-harvest.kyriewen.cn
📧 Support: support@kyriewen.cn
```

## 简体中文🔍 智能图片提取

```text

自动识别网页上的所有图片 —— 包括标准图片、CSS 背景图、响应式图片和高清变体。深入扫描 Shadow DOM 和 iframe，确保不遗漏任何一张图。Pro 用户还可开启实时监控，滚动页面时自动发现新加载的图片。

🎛️ 强大的筛选与排序

按尺寸（小/中/大/超大/自定义）、格式（JPG/PNG/WebP/SVG/GIF）、比例（方形/横图/竖图/全景）或 URL 关键词灵活筛选。智能分组支持按域名、格式、尺寸范围或标签页分类（Pro）。可按大小、格式或原始顺序排序。开启「仅显示可见」可聚焦当前屏幕上的图片。

📥 批量下载图片

支持单张下载或多张打包为 ZIP 一键下载。可视化进度条实时追踪下载进度。免费用户每次可批量下载最多 30 张图片；Pro 用户享受无限量批量下载。

🌐 多标签页提取

一次性从多个浏览器标签页中提取图片。无需逐个切换 —— 同时扫描所有已打开的标签页，一键下载全部图片。非常适合跨页面的调研、购物比价或内容整理工作。

🔎 相似图片检测

基于先进的感知哈希算法，自动检测并归组视觉上相似的图片。避免下载重复或高度相似的变体图片，在下载前识别冗余图片，节省时间和存储空间。

🔍 以图搜图

快速查找某张图片在网上的其他出处。免费用户可使用 Google 图片搜索。Pro 用户解锁 4 大搜索引擎：Google 图片、TinEye、百度识图和 Yandex —— 全方位追踪图片来源、寻找更高分辨率版本或验证图片真实性。

✨ 批量高亮定位

在面板中选中多张图片，即可在网页上同时高亮显示它们。自动滚动到每张高亮图片的位置，帮你快速定位和确认。下载前批量预览大量图片的理想工具。

⭐ 图片收藏夹

将心仪的图片保存到本地收藏，支持自定义标签和备注。随时浏览、搜索和筛选已保存的图片。支持整个收藏批量导出为 ZIP 文件。无需离开浏览器，即可打造你的个人图片库。

🎨 色彩提取

自动提取每张图片的 5 种主色调，在面板中直接查看色彩盘。一键复制 HEX 色值，方便用于设计工作、品牌分析或创意项目。

🤖 AI 智能标注

借助 AI 为任意图片生成智能标签 —— 无需打开图片即可了解其内容。支持单张标注或批量标注选中的所有图片。通过 AI 标签筛选和搜索图片。免费用户每月享有一定配额；Pro 用户不限量使用。

🦅 导出到 Eagle

将图片直接发送到 Eagle —— 热门的设计素材管理工具。每次导出自动携带图片、元数据、AI 标签和来源 URL，让你的 Eagle 素材库井然有序。支持批量导出所有选中图片。免费用户可导出最多 5 张；Pro 用户无限量导出。

⚡ 批量操作

选中多张图片后可批量执行操作：批量收藏、批量 AI 标注、批量删除。告别逐一点击 —— 面对大量图片时大幅提升效率。

🌍 支持 15 种语言

完整翻译为 15 种语言：English、简体中文、繁體中文、日本語、Español、한국어、Português、Français、Deutsch、Italiano、Русский、Nederlands、Polski、العربية、ไทย。自动检测浏览器语言，无需手动设置。

🔒 隐私优先

Image Harvest 在浏览器本地完成所有处理。图片、网址和个人数据绝不会离开你的设备。可在「设置 → 帮助改进」中选择开启匿名使用统计（仅统计事件次数，不含任何网址、图片或身份信息），默认关闭。

⌨️ 快捷键

Ctrl+Shift+S（Mac 上为 Cmd+Shift+S）：打开/关闭 Image Harvest

🌐 官网：https://image-harvest.kyriewen.cn
```
